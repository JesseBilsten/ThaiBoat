Business Hours

OPEN: Monday through Friday

  • Lunch: 11:00am — 2:00pm
  • Dinner: 4:30pm — 8:00pm

CLOSED: Saturday and Sunday

WARNING: Call first!
Thai Boat will close randomly and without notice sometimes due to the family travelling back to Thailand. It's best to call before making the drive and if you get the answering machine it's a pretty safe bet they're closed.

Please Note

Spicy "Spicy" Dishes may be ordered mild, medium, hot or very hot.

  • Extra meat or vegetables add $2
  • NO substitute item

  • We are not responsible for lost or stolen items

  • We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone
